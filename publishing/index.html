So you want to publish data on Raleigh OpenData? We have just a handful of steps to get you started.

  • Make sure your data isn't already published on the portal
  • Sign up for Raleigh OpenData Account (if you haven't already)
  • Review the publishing guidelines (these change based on feedback)
  • Download and complete the Publisher's Packet (Excel Workbook)
    Before filling out the Publisher's Packet, make sure to enable editing and macros. Excel will likely prompt you once for each. The packet contains detailed guidance you can follow along. Fill out all the required fields. The workbook will track your progress toward completion.
  • Click "Submit this Packet!" in your completed workbook
    You can find this button in the Publisher's Packet at the bottom of either the tab '1 - Instructions' or '5 - Automation Questionnaire'. It will save a copy of your packet with a new name, open an email in your email client and the folder containing your saved packet. Just attach the copy to the email and click send.

Someone from the open data team will follow up with you shortly to guide you through the rest of the process. Don't worry, it's not complicated. Think of us as your data sherpas guiding you through your journey to published data.
